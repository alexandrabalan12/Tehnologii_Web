Our Mission

Our Story

At PlaCo, our foundations are built on a legacy of passion and precision. Our journey began in the spring of 2024 when CO-Founders Balan Alexandra and Roman Andra, two hard-working students from FII, got assigned this very interested project.
Starting with a small, dedicated team, we tackled our first projects with a blend of youthful exuberance and strategic expertise. Each home we built wasn't just a structure but a canvas where we painted our clients' dreams.
